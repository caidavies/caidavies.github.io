Support the team not the board

Action For Albion is a fan led protest group to highlight the grave concerns we have surrounding the ownership of our great club, West Bromwich Albion.

About us

Who we are

Action For Albion was formed due to unsatisfactory communication from the club's controlling shareholder Guochuan Lai and Chief Executive Ron Gourlay regarding financial matters.

Since the group was formed, there have been a series of worrying financial developments, that have not been communicated properly to the fans of the club.

Our aim, is to raise awareness to the gross mismanagement of West Bromwich Albion through legal and peaceful protests. In addition, we would like to enable a dialogue between the custodians of the football club and its fans.

We will always support the team and coaching staff and never take actions that could harm results on the pitch. Our protests will remain structured, legal and peaceful.

Our approach

How will you protest?
We will always protest peacefully and legally. We are not an angry mob and do not condone any form of violence, racism, homophobia, transphobia or misogyny from our members.
Can I help?
We are always looking for fans to join, please Contact Us on the form below, or through Twitter, or Facebook to get involved.
When is your next protest?
We aim to regularly protest at both home and away matches. Follow our social accounts to see regular updates.
Won't protesting affect the team on the pitch?
We will never protest against the team and do not believe our protests will affect the team's performance in any way.
